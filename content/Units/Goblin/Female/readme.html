Female Goblin

Model by: Dan van Ohllus (aka Kuhneghetz)

Descriptions:
A female goblin.
This model could be useful as a villager, an NPC or maybe a tier 1 unit if you add some weapon attachments.

For the facial and body structure, I was a little inspired from the female goblins in World of Warcraft, especially the smaller upward crooked nose and the botox lips.

Model descriptions:
The entire goblin mesh is heavily modified from the Harpy.
Animations taken from Demoness, Sylvanas, Ogre, Watcher/Runner and Grunt.
External portrait model included with Demoness Portrait animations.
100% ingame textures.

YOU MUST GIVE ME CREDITS IF YOU USE THIS MODEL IN YOUR MAP!!!

Feel free to edit this model for your projects as long as you credit me for the model you based it from.

MDX files can have any path, though the portrait model must be in the same path as the main model.

Enjoy!

HINT: You'll might need to re-scale her in world editor to 0.60 or 0.65 if you wish to make her as small as the other goblins.
