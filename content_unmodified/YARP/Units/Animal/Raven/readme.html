Descriptions:
The raven (can also be used as a crow) has a good use for various types of maps, such as myths, stories and even real-life-type maps.

Based on the War Eagle model (one of Beastmaster's summons). It uses a texture from a raven, wich can be seen in the Orc Campaignscreen. The legs' texture comes from Druid Storm Crow Form with darker tinting. So this model uses in-game textures.
Portrait camera is built in the model.
