Descriptions:
A queen is a qualifying reference to a female monarch, possessing and exercising all of the monarchical powers of a ruler.
This interpretion of a queen is inspired by an amount of queens from the victorian era, thus she has that pale make-up on her face.

Model descriptions:
Head and upper body is based on Villager Woman. Crown, collar, skirt and scepter are made from scratch.
Animations taken from wc3 units.
Portrait model is included.
100% ingame textures.
